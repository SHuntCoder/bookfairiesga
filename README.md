# 🦋 Book Fairies

**Creating Passion One Page at a Time**

Book Fairies is a student-led community literacy nonprofit based in Fulton County, Georgia, founded by **Marin DuMond and Lauren Holbrook**. We collect and distribute books to underserved students and families across Fulton County school communities — because every child deserves the joy of reading.

---

## 🌸 Our Mission

Book Fairies is dedicated to expanding access to books and promoting literacy for children in underserved communities throughout Fulton County. By organizing book drives and community partnerships, we help place books directly into the hands of students who need them most.

We believe books inspire imagination, confidence, education, and opportunity — and every child deserves access to those opportunities.

---

## 📚 What We Do

- **Community Book Drives** — We organize book drives throughout schools, neighborhoods, and local organizations to collect new and gently used books for children of all ages.
- **Book Distribution** — Donated books are carefully sorted and distributed to underserved schools, community centers, and youth programs throughout Fulton County.
- **Volunteer Opportunities** — Students and community members can help organize donations, sort books, and support literacy events while earning volunteer service hours.
- **Literacy Advocacy** — We work to raise awareness about childhood literacy and the importance of equal access to educational resources.

---

## 📦 How to Donate Books

1. **Find any books** for ages 4–16 — new or gently used
2. **Schedule a pickup** — pickups are available almost every Sunday *(Milton / Alpharetta area only)*
3. **We handle the rest** — your books will be distributed to underserved schools across Fulton County
