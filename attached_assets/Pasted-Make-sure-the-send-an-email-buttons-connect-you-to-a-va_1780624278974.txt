Our Mission
Book Fairies is dedicated to expanding access to books and promoting literacy for children in underserved communities throughout Fulton County. By organizing book drives and community partnerships, we help place books directly into the hands of students who need them most.

We believe books inspire imagination, confidence, education, and opportunity — and every child deserves access to those opportunities.

Meet the Founders
Marin DuMond & Lauren Holbrook
Book Fairies was founded by Marin DuMond and Lauren Holbrook with a shared goal of making reading more accessible to children throughout the community.

After recognizing that many students lacked access to books outside the classroom, we created Book Fairies to help bridge that gap through donation drives, volunteer efforts, and community connections. Our mission is simple: help every child build a love for reading and create a joy for learning.

What We Do
Community Book Drives
We organize book drives throughout schools, neighborhoods, and local organizations to collect new and gently used books for children of all ages.

Book Distribution
Donated books are carefully sorted and distributed to underserved schools, community centers, and youth programs throughout Fulton County.

Volunteer Opportunities
Students and community members can help organize donations, sort books, and support literacy events while earning volunteer service hours.

Literacy Advocacy
We work to raise awareness about childhood literacy and the importance of equal access to educational resources.

FAQ Section
Where are the books donated?
Books are distributed to underserved schools, students, and community organizations throughout Fulton County.

Can students volunteer?
Yes! Student volunteers are welcome and may earn community service hours through participation. Email bookfairiesgeorgia@gmail.comto request opportunities. 

What books are most needed?
We take all books! Children’s books, early readers, middle school novels, and educational materials are always appreciated.

How can organizations partner with Book Fairies?
Schools, businesses, and local organizations can contact us to organize donation drives or literacy initiatives.

How do I donate?

As of now, book donations drop on a monthly basis and are available almost all Sundays in the month. From there sign the pick up form for your preferred date, and let us take care of the rest!
